Interactive RL Demo — Q-learning Gridworld

This sandbox trains a tiny Q-learning agent on a gridworld. Tweak learning-rate, epsilon, episodes and watch the agent learn to reach the goal. It's all client-side — fast and editable.

Tip: training many episodes will run faster if you increase the episodes slider and press Train. Q-values are visualized by arrows and cell heat.
